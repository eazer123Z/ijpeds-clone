Author Guidelines

  • Manuscripts must be original, not published or under review elsewhere
  • Use the IJPEDS manuscript template (available for download)
  • Abstract: structured (Background, Methods, Results, Conclusion), max 300 words
  • Keywords: 3-6 relevant keywords
  • References: Vancouver style, max 40 for original articles, 60 for reviews
  • Figures: minimum 300 DPI, JPEG or TIFF format
  • Tables: editable format (not images)
  • Ethics: IRB approval and informed consent for human studies
  • Conflict of Interest: all authors must declare conflicts
  • Authorship: follow ICMJE criteria for authorship

Article Types & Word Limits

  • Original Article: 3000-4000 words, max 6 tables/figures, max 40 references
  • Review Article: 4000-6000 words, max 8 tables/figures, max 60 references
  • Case Report: 1500-2000 words, max 4 tables/figures, max 20 references
  • Letter to Editor: 500-800 words, max 5 references, max 1 figure/table
  • Editorial: By invitation only, 1000-1500 words
  • Commentary: 1000-2000 words, max 20 references
