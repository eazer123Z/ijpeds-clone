About the Journal

International Journal of Pediatrics and Adolescent Medicine

About IJPEDS

The International Journal of Pediatrics (IJPEDS) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal that publishes original research, review articles, case reports, and letters to the editor in all areas of pediatric medicine and child health.

Established in 2015, IJPEDS is committed to advancing pediatric healthcare through the dissemination of high-quality research. Our editorial board comprises leading pediatric specialists from around the world, ensuring rigorous peer review and global relevance.

Scope & Aims

The journal covers a broad range of topics including but not limited to:

  • General Pediatrics and Adolescent Medicine
  • Neonatology and Perinatal Medicine
  • Pediatric Surgery and Subspecialties
  • Childhood Infectious Diseases and Vaccination
  • Pediatric Nutrition and Growth
  • Developmental and Behavioral Pediatrics
  • Pediatric Genetics and Rare Diseases
  • Child and Adolescent Mental Health
  • Pediatric Emergency and Critical Care
  • Global Child Health and Public Health

Publication Ethics

IJPEDS adheres to the guidelines set by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE). All submitted manuscripts are screened for plagiarism using automated tools. Authors must confirm that their work is original, not under consideration elsewhere, and approved by all co-authors.

Studies involving human subjects must include ethics committee approval and informed consent documentation. Clinical trials must be registered in a recognized public registry.
