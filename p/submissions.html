Author Guidelines

  • Title: max 10 words
  • Abstract: max 200 words, self-explanatory
  • Structure: Introduction - Method - Results and Discussion - Conclusion
  • References: min 10-25, in order [1], [2], [3]
  • Figures: numbered, captioned below
  • Tables: numbered, captioned above
