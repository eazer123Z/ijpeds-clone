About the Journal

Focus and Scope

The International Journal of Pediatrics (IJPEDS) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal publishing original research, reviews, case reports, and letters in all areas of pediatric medicine and child health.

Peer Review Process

Double-blind peer review by at least two reviewers. Average review: 4-6 weeks.

Publication Frequency

Quarterly (4 issues/year).

Open Access

Immediate open access under CC BY license.

Publication Ethics

IJPEDS adheres to COPE guidelines. All manuscripts screened for plagiarism.
