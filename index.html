The International Journal of Pediatrics (IJPEDS), p-ISSN: XXXX-XXXX, e-ISSN: XXXX-XXXX, is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to advancing pediatric healthcare through the dissemination of high-quality research. The journal covers all areas of pediatric medicine and child health including general pediatrics, neonatology, pediatric surgery, infectious diseases, nutrition, genetics, and adolescent medicine.

IJPEDS uses a rolling submission process, allowing authors to submit at any time during the year. The journal is indexed in Scopus, PubMed Central, DOAJ, and EBSCO.

Policy on Multiple Submission (Simultaneous Submissions) and Prior Publication

Posted: 2025-11-06

Dear Authors, The International Journal of Pediatrics is committed to upholding the highest standards of academic integrity and publishing ethics. Manuscripts submitted must be original, unpublished, and not under consideration elsewhere. Submitting the same manuscript to more than one journal simultaneously is strictly prohibited. Authors who violate this policy may be subject to formal warnings, publication bans, or retraction.
